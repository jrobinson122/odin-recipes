Impossible Burger ohhhh yeahhhhh

Description

Ever since I stopped eating red meat, I have been looking for a good burger alternative. It felt like there was a void in my life. Recently, I have discovered that Impossible has filled that void! This vegetarian option has proven to scratch an itch that has been bothering me for a while. It is a great alternative and you should definitely give it a try!

Ingredients

  • 12 Ounce Impossible Beef
  • 1 Cup Mayo
  • 2 Tablespoons Sriracha
  • 2 Tablespoons Minced Peppeoncini
  • 2 Tablespoons Ketchup
  • 2 TableSpoons Dill Pickle Relish
  • Salt and Pepper
  • American Cheese
  • Brioche Buns
  • Butter Lettuce
  • Tomato Slices
  • Onion Slices
  • Dill Pickle Chips

Recipe

1. Make the Americana Sauce

  1. Mince Pepperoncini
  2. Combine with mayo, ketchup, sriracha, and dill pickle relish

2. Cook Impossible Beef

  1. Season impossible meat with salt and pepper
  2. Cook over medium high heat for 2-3 minutes on each side
  3. Add slice of american cheese on top and cover until melted

3. Assemble Burger

  1. Lightly toast buns
  2. Add layer of americana sauce
  3. Top with burger, lettuce, tomato, onion, and dill pickle chips
